Home Access Scrapper

A scrapper of Home Access Center that lets you see your grades from a terminal

Developed in JavaScript (NodeJS)


This project is a recent undertaking of mine, where I use Home Access Center (HAC) to pull grades and display them in a more interesting way. I find HAC's interface to be very out of date, and also very clunky to use, especially with seeing past grades. I aim to fix this in the future by modernizing the display and allowing for more features, such as grade predictions, a track of progress, and possible reccomendations of studying using AI, however this will be incredibly difficult, mainly just trying to get the information was challenging enough, as my scrapping skills are not the best, and HAC's source code is just not the best, and I have no clue on how to gain access to grades from past grading periods. Heres to hoping I figure it out soon.
